Derk Janssen Poetry Contest

Sponsored by Mr. Benson and the AcaDec class at PHS, the Memorial Derk Janssen Poetry Contest is a commemoration of Mr. Janssen's immensely excellent poetry that we have all come to love. Contest rules are available on the "rules" page; to submit your piece of poetry, click on the "submit" page. There are prizes!
