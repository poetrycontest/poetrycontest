Derk Janssen Memorial Poetry Contest

Sponsored by Mr. Benson and the AcaDec class at PHS, the poetry is a commemoration of Derk Janssen's incredibly excellent, exhilarating poetry that many of us have come to love. Contest rules are available on the "rules" page; to submit your piece of poetry, click on the "submit" page. There are prizes!
