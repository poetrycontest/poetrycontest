Contest Rules

Prizes: 1st Place $100, 2nd Place $50, 3rd Place $25.

Please Note

Your poem must be submitted by Aprill 11th and incorporate this year's theme- "Celebration of Life"- to be judged. We prefer poems to be submitted here but you may also turn in a paper copy with your student ID.

Your poem must adhere to the following in order to be eligible to receive those awesome prizes:

1. Be an orginial work of your own writing without any plagarism;

2. Not be obscene nor crude in any way;

3. Be submitted within the time alotted- one poem entry per person.

4. Follow this year's (inagural) theme: Celebration of Life.
